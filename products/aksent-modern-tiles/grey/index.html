Norstone Grey 3D Basalt

Capture the Precision & Clean Lines of the Grey Basalt Aksent 3D

This color can fit well with a wide palette of cooler tones found most commonly in Contemporary Design. The Aksent 3D Tile itself is constructed to work particularly well for larger feature walls where long, refined lines are desired.
