NATURAL STONE SIDING FOR EVERY LANDSCAPE

Ochre XL Rock Panel ™

Part of our high performance natural stone veneer wall panels, Ochre XL Rock Panels are formed from a variety of types of natural stone, all belonging to the quartz-sedimentary family. Through their unique shape and material, the XL Rock Panels lock together to create a stunning, seamless landscape stone facade. Speed and a seamliess finish are the hallmarks of our XL Collection where Ochre is the most popular color of the group.

Designed for Elegance, Built to Perform.

Meticulously shaped by hand into 6" x 24" Units, Ochre XL Stone Veneer Rock Panels install efficinetly and - unlike other thin veneer panels in the market - precisely. Each panel is carefully crafted and checked against a rigorous set of quality control standards to ensure the product you get installs right the first time.
