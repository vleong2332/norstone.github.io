MAKE A STATEMENT WITH NATURAL STONE IN YOUR KITCHEN.

Stone on More than just the Countertop

There's good reason that natural stone, such as granite, marble, and quartz, are the most sought after kitchen countertop products - they're both beautiful and durable. But wait - what about the rest of your kitchen? Don't leave the wall spaces of your kitchen islands, half walls, over the cabinet space, and breakfast nooks barren. Consider using a product like Norstone Rock Panels to create a stacked stone kitchen feature wall that will be unique and durable.

Where We Gather

The kitchen often serves as the center of the home, where we eat, do homework, pay bills, and check email. Create a warm and welcoming environment with a stacked stone kitchen feature wall that will inspire you and your family and have your friends and relatives asking where you got the stone when they visit.

Practical Considerations

A Natural Stone kitchen wall should be sealed with a quality water based natural stone sealer when it is installed and re-sealed per the sealer manufacturer's recommendations. The sealer will form a protective barrier on the stone and help keep the wall clean during life's day to day activities.
