Norstone is Transforming the Way Designers See Stone.

Natural Stone meant just for interior. 

Norstone has advanced the concept of interior stone walls. By designing a tile that is 100% natural stone but which is still light-weight and simple-to-install, we have become the Interior Designers' product of choice for feature walls in Restaurants, Residences, and Commercial Office Buildings. This simple concept of natural but light-weight allows one to feature real stone in ways never before possible.

We encourage you to push the boundaries with us too.

Rock Panel Collection

Norstone rock panels offer the textural beauty of natural stacked stone in easy-to-install, modular veneers for interior and exterior walls. Each panel is handmade from select pieces of cut quartz or quartzite-based stone, offering one-of-a-kind natural color variations. Norstone’s rock panels provide enduring style in durable, versatile three-dimensional designs suitable for residential and commercial projects of all sizes.

Rock Panels are often used for:
  • Feature walls
  • Pool surrounds
  • Courtyards
  • Entertainment areas
  • Stone fireplaces

XL Rock Panels

Bigger wall projects demand bolder designs, and Norstone has you covered with oversize, interlocking rock panels. XL panels have the same hand-stacked quality as all of our rock panels, but use stones that are twice the size for greater visual impact. Like the other panels, they come in the same convenient 6” x 24” size and also feature our proprietary “finger-jointed” corner system.

For larger projects, XL Rock Panels are perfect for:
  • 2-story fireplaces
  • Landscape walls
  • Facades
  • Pools
  • Commercial entertainment areas

Basalt Tiles

Our newest offering of interlocking lava (IL) tiles can elevate your project to new heights of innovative styling. Norstone’s precision crafting, coupled with our advanced technology, delivers a sleek, smooth surface ideal for making an impressive design statement. Basalt IL tiles can be used indoors or out, and are popular for kitchens, baths and feature walls.

Basalt Lava tiles are designed for:
  • Modern backsplashes
  • Bathrooms
  • Feature walls
  • Pools
  • Landscape walls
  • Water features
